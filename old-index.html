Career Goals

Wildlife Biology

I am passionate about using technology to advance the field of Wildlife Biology. There is so much data from the past and the present that could be analyzed to help us deepen our understanding of our country's natural history, and I would love to build the tools necessary to get through that backlog.

I am interested in utilizing data science to help clean and analyze data in the Wildlife Biology field. I am using my Python and Web Development skills to brainstorm and build apps to help with data collection, cleaning, access and presentation. I like programming in Python because of the simplicity of the language as well as the vast number of resources provided by the open source Python community.
